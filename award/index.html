Award

The Young Author Best Paper Entropy Award honors an excellent paper presented at the Max Ent 2018, which is distinguished by its originality, importance of the topic and quality of presentation. This award is offered by the Entropy journal.

In order for a paper to be considered for the Max Ent 2018 Young Author Best Paper Entropy Award, the following three criteria must be met:

  • The candidate has to be 30 years of age or younger on 1 January 2019.
  • The candidate has to be the principal author of the paper.
  • The candidate has to be the presenting author of the paper.

If your paper meets the above eligibility criteria and you wish it to be considered for the Max Ent 2018 Young Author Best Paper Entropy Award, please indicate it in the registration form.

Prof. Emma McCoy will chair the Award Committee and select the best paper among those eligible based on the quality of the paper (including originality of results and potential impact) as well as on the quality of the presentation.

The nominated authors and the award winner will be announced during the conference.
